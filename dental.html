The problem

You're the best dentist
in your clinic.
And that's part of the problem.

Your schedule has no room for managing.

The chair is full from 9 to 2 and 4 to 8.

In between: sterilisation, quotes, calls, team WhatsApps.

And the clinic decisions no one can make without you.

Not a lack of time. You're just indispensable for too many things at once.

Your team waits for you, even when they don't have to.

A dental assistant doesn't know whether to accept a case without a confirmed quote.

The receptionist doesn't know how to handle the patient threatening to leave.

The hygienist doesn't know whether to refer without checking with you.

It's not that they don't know. They don't have your judgment. And you can't interrupt a root canal to give it to them.

Alter CEO · Dental solves that.

What it is

Not a protocol.
Your judgment.

Alter CEO · Dental captures how you think and decide — how you manage your team, suppliers, difficult patients, unpaid bills, hiring — and makes it available for whoever you choose.

It doesn't answer with generic clinic management protocols.
It answers the way you would.

The difference between a protocol and your judgment is that a protocol was written by someone who doesn't know you. The clone knows you.

01

Your context, not the generic one

It knows your suppliers, your team and your lines you never cross. It doesn't answer from theory — it answers from your reality.

02

Available when you're not

At 8:45 before opening. At 2:10 after the last patient. On a Saturday if there's an emergency. Judgment doesn't have a chair.

03

No interruptions

Your team consults before acting. You don't get the WhatsApp mid-filling. The decision comes out right without you having to step in.

04

Confidential

The profile is yours. Only those you authorise can access it. Nothing leaves your clinic.

The process

Three steps.
No endless meetings.

01

Capture your judgment

In two or three sessions with you, we build your decision profile. How you manage the team, what you do when a supplier fails, how you handle a difficult patient, what you never delegate. Not an interview — a conversation with purpose.

02

Setup and testing

We configure the clone with your profile and test it with you using real cases from your clinic. You validate every response. If something doesn't fit, we adjust it until it does.

03

Your team uses it

Whoever you authorise can consult the clone at any time. No new apps. A chat conversation, just like they'd message you. The difference is the clone always responds.
